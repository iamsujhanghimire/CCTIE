OUR MISSION

Our mission is to harvest technological, innovative, and entrepreneurial talent and use it for our community’s benefit while also placing our members in a position to succeed, both personally and professionally.

At TIE, we "learn to grow or grow by learning, these are the only choices". Every step in life is a growth process that we have learnt to embrace. With that, we would like to educate, innovate and inspire one another.

OUR VALUES

BALANCE

Achieving and maintaining a balance between a professional and social atmosphere in order to produce an efficient organization.

LEARNING

Continuously challenging yourself and others to grow and adapt to change in order to apply learned knowledge to any situation.

STUDENT FOCUS

Creating fun and innovative programs by the students, for the students in order to satisfy their needs and develop a holistic individual.

TEAMWORK

Building a positive foundation by mentoring, communicating and cooperating with each other in order to achieve goals and become a powerful organization.
